What will you discover?

We are a deliciously authentic vegetarian Thali cafe with a social conscience. We cater for all sorts of dietery needs, such as vegan, gluten and nut free. You can find us in Hackney, East London.

Find out more

Ethos

Our aim is not only to produce the most deliciously authentic thali, but to minimise our impact on the environment in getting to your plate. We believe passionately that food should not be wasted, so we ensure that absolutely every foodscrap will be utilised or composted.


Responsibility

By dining at The Thali Rooms you will be contributing towards our annual charitable donation to Action Against Hunger. We ensure that 10% of our yearly profits go directly to feeding those in need, so the more diners we have, the bigger our donation will be.

Taste

We care about quality, locally sourced, seasonal produce. Our menu is constantly shifting to reflect the seasons. Book now to see what The Thali Rooms is really all about.
