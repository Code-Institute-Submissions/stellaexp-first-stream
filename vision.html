Our Vision

When we started this journey it was really important to not only replicate the most authentic Thali you could have outside of India, but to contribute positively to towards Action Against Hunger. We ensure all our paying diners that we contribute 10% of our profits to AAH and we have rotating dishes on the menu of which a higher proportion of profit will go towards the charity. You can find these on the menu marked by an asterix.

*

By dining at The Thali Rooms you will help the fight to end world hunger.
