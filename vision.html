Our Vision

When we started this journey it was really important to not only replicate the most authentic Thali you could have outside of India, but to contribute positively to towards Action Against Hunger. We ensure all our paying diners that we contribute 10% of our profits to AAH and we have rotating dishes on the menu of which a higher proportion of profit will go towards the charity. Ask your server for details.

Our three main ethos

Environmental
We're commited to looking after the environment and we don't offer single use plastics, all takeouts come in biodegradable Vegware. As for our ingredients, we have a strict no air freight policy to help reduce our carbon footprint. We compost all our food scraps and actively fight food waste.
Sustainability
We only use seasonal produce, which is ethically sourced. We pride ourselves on our close relationship with our suppliers and offer traceability on all our ingredients. Our menu is always changing based on what is currently in season, if you have any questions on this please ask your server.
Community
Community is at the heart of what we do, as part of our effort to reduce food waste, we donate spare meals to local homeless charities. We also run monthly workshops on topics such as food waste and upcycling - see our notice board for details.
